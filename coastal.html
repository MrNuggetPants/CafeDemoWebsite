Our story

A cafe at the edge of the harbour

Tide & Cup has been on the Padstow waterfront since 2016. The name comes from a simple truth: the rhythm of the cafe follows the tide. When the boats come in, the fishermen come in. When the tourists arrive in summer, we open the terrace.

We serve St Austell roasted coffee alongside proper Cornish food — things you'd want to eat with a view of the water. Nothing fancy, just good ingredients and fresh air.

Dogs welcome. Children welcome. Just mind the seagulls.
