About us

A room worth sitting in

Stillwater started in 2018 in a first-floor flat on Dublin Street. The space was small, the menu was smaller, and somehow that was exactly right.

We moved to our current location in 2021 — a converted Georgian reading room with original plasterwork and north-facing windows that fill the space with a particular kind of light. We try not to get in its way.

The coffee comes from Square Mile in London. The food comes from farmers we've been working with since the beginning. Nothing is over-complicated.

Stillwater is the kind of cafe you hope exists everywhere and find almost nowhere.

The Scotsman
