Our story

A cafe built from the ground up

The Hearthside opened its doors ten years ago in a converted Victorian railway building. The exposed beams and original brick walls were already there — we just listened to what the space wanted to be.

We source all our beans from a single family farm in the highlands of Ethiopia, roasted weekly by a small cooperative in Bristol. Every coffee you drink here has a name attached to it.

Food is cooked in-house from scratch each morning. The menu changes with what's good and seasonal — but the brown butter banana bread never leaves.
